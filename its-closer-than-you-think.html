It's Closer Than You Think.

I get insanely creative in a constrained environment.

Thursday, May 26, 2022

It takes a lot of effort for one to accept his/her problems, to do what what seems impossible. Fortunately, for those who have learned to just accept whatever life throws at us and deal with it, also know that nothing worthwhile comes without a little bit of suffering and or struggle.

Over the couple of months that passed, I learned so much from myself and from others. I am forever grateful for all those I have met, friends I have and my family. It feels releaving/beautiful to have people you love and who love you, accepting you for who you are.

This day is very special because it marks the day Christ ascends to Heaven. As for me, it marks a new start. Constant learning and improvement to get closer to what I want. I feel very lucky to have been able to wake up today and share this thoughts of mine.

Over his lifetime Jesus showed empathy, humility and brotherhood. He gave more than he took and did the impossible. This is proof that nothing is impossible with him with us.

The holy bible teaches us so much of the ways to live our lives. So much that having a grasp of the principles of living in the scriptures make everything else seem simply not new.

It simply makes sense to smile even in the hardest moments. It simple makes sense to, when every body else is giving up, to give you're all in - because it's precisely at that point that the difference lies.

We tend to try hard to fit in society - to find our place amongst humans that we forget to look up to what comes after - and that we were born to standout.
